# About Velocity

Velocity is the high-performance rendering engine designed for next-generation creative applications. We enable developers to build scalable, personalized video experiences without managing complex infrastructure.

## Our Mission

To democratize access to high-scale video rendering, allowing creative teams to focus on design while we handle the heavy lifting of processing and delivery.

## Why Velocity?

*   **Scalability**: Built to handle millions of concurrent renders.
*   **Speed**: Optimized for near-real-time generation.
*   **Reliability**: Enterprise-grade infrastructure with 99.99% uptime.
